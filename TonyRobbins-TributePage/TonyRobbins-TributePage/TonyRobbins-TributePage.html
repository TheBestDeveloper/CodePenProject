Tony Robbins

Author Entrepreneur, Philanthropist and Life Coach

Energetic Tony during a seminar

Here's the timeline of Tony Robbins:

  • Childhood And Early Life
    • Born in February 29, 1960 as Anthony J. Mahavorick in North Hollywood, California.
  • Career
    • Started as a promoter for Jim Rohn’s motivational seminars
    • Got training in NPL and Ericksonian hyponsis from Milton Hyland Erickson. Later, in 1983 he learnt to firewalk from Tolly Burkan, father of the global firewalking movement, and started integrating it into his seminars.
    • His career as a self-help guru started to escalate when he started selling his programs through books and TV infomercials. He popularized himself as a ‘peak performance coach’ and gained a lot of recognition.
    • Published his Major book - "Unlimited Power" in 1987.
    • His other famous books are : "Awaken the Giant Within" - 1991 , "Giant Steps" - 1994 , "Notes from a friend" -1995
    • In 1991 he launched "Anthony Robbins Foundation" to empower students and prisoners through food drives and learning programs based on his teachings.
    • In 1997 he started his "Leadership Academy" seminar. With the success of these seminars, he became the featured speaker on the seminar circuit sponsored by Learning Annex
    • He became the featured speaker at the 2007 TED conference. His talk was the 6th most popular TED talk by 2012.
    • He founded "Robbins-Madanes Center for Strategic Intervention" , which deals with personal, family and organizational psychology.
    • In 2009, he founded "Business Mastery program" , focusing on endowing business leaders with techniques to manage and grow their busines
    • Robbins acted in "The Singularity Is Near: A True Story about The Future" in 2010
  • Major Work
    • Robbins’ four-day seminar "Unleash the Power Within" is considered to be the most prominent work of his career. The seminar is based on the concept of how successful people overcome their fears. ‘Mastery University’ is another successful work of his.
  • Personal Life and Legacy
    • Robbins' got married to Becky Jenkins in 1982. Becky had 3 children from her former marriages; Robbins adopted them. They got divorced after 9 years of being married. He got married for the second time to Sage Robbins.
  • Trivia
    • Met and interacted with famous personalities like Nelson Mandela, Mikhail Gorbachev, Bill Clinton, Margaret Thatcher, Francois Mitterrand, and Princess Diana
    • Robbins was diagnosed with brain tumor in 1994 but he got it removed soon after.
    • He has a son, Jairek Robbins, with his former girlfriend Liz Acosta.
    • In 2001, Robbins was called a ‘hypocrite’ by ‘The Vancouver Sun’. In retaliation, he filed a lawsuit against the publication, alleging defamation and libel. He was awarded US$ 20,000 in compensation.
